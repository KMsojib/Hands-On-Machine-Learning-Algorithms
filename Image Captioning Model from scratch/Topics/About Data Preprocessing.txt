/* ----------------------------------- Data Cleaning -----------------------------------*/
Def :- Clean data improved the quality of result and enhances the performances of dat modeling.
    (i) Missing values :- Using strategies like mean/mode imputation, deletion, or predictive models to fill in or remove missing data.
    (ii) Removing duplicates :-  Eliminating duplicate records to ensure each entry is unique and relevant.
    (iii) Correcting inconsistent formats :-  Standardizing formats (e.g., date formats, string cases) to maintain consistency.
